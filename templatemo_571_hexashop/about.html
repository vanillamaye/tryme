About Us & Our Skills

Meet Marielle Lois P. Bahuyo, the visionary mind and proud owner of ThreadRhapsody, an enchanting online shopping haven where affordability meets impeccable quality. Marielle draws inspiration from the exquisite fashion worlds curated by style icons like Heart Evangelista and Ms. Yannick. As a dedicated student of fashion, Marielle has honed her skills by attentively watching and learning the intricate details of the ever-evolving world of style. With each carefully curated piece, Marielle aims not only to showcase her design prowess but also to inspire others to embrace their creativity and unique sense of style. Her journey as a student of fashion has evolved into a story of determination, artistic expression, and the belief that everyone has the ability to design their own narrative.
